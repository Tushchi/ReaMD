[SIZE=5][B]ReaMD - Markdown Viewer with Scenario Linking, Teleprompter & AI Parse[/B][/SIZE]

Hey everyone,

I work in podcast production, radio dramas, commercials, and general radio sound design - with occasional dubbing projects on the side. For years I wanted a tool that could clean up messy scripts from producers and clients, and also serve as a simple teleprompter for voice sync sessions. With the help of Claude AI, I finally built it. Hope it helps you as much as it helps me!

[IMG]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/b451c/ReaMD/main/docs/images/hero.png[/IMG]

[B]What is ReaMD?[/B]

ReaMD is a dockable markdown viewer designed for audio production workflows. It bridges the gap between your scripts, notes, and the timeline - allowing you to link text fragments directly to items on your tracks.

Perfect for [B]voiceover production[/B], [B]audio post-production[/B], [B]podcast editing[/B], and [B]music production[/B] (lyrics, arrangement notes).
